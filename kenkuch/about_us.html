About Us

Services

Kenkuch Electical/Electronics Konsult renders professional services and technical assistance in Electrical and electronics in the following.

1. We build/assemble battery chargers and Inverters.

2. Coiling of transformers, power Supply Unit.

3. We teach and train in Electronics components P.C.B's and soldering techniques

4. We install CCTV Cameras, Inverters, Solar Systems ranging from

  • Solar bore hole
  • Solar Billboard
  • Solar Street Lights

We also produce circuit diagrams and repairs of UPS, Inverters and other Electronic devices. We are into public address systems and installations. we have the capacity to undertake and execute projects and contracts of both Government and private establishments. just as our name implies, we can be consulted for any Electrical/Electronic issues

Vision

To be a leading home grown Electrical/Electronics help-mate to our teeming youths and Nigeria in general. Also to be a house hold name in Electrical and Electronic pratical trainings for students and technical know-how to excel in life

Mission

To provide altenative power source in a challenging weak power circumstances and to provide training and empowerment to the nigeria youths. In addition to offer technical service and assitance as it concerns Electrical and Electronics projeces and related field.

To build a generation of creative minds through professional handling, thus creating employement and reduction in over-dependency
